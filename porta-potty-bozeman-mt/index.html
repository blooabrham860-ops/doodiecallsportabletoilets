Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often do you clean and service the portable toilets?

For all rentals, service is scheduled at least once a week. For high-traffic events or long-term construction projects, we can create a customized service schedule to meet your specific needs, including multiple services per week to ensure units remain clean and fully stocked.

2. What is included in the porta potty rental prices in USA?

Our rental cost of a porta potty includes the delivery of the unit, placement at your desired location, all necessary supplies like toilet paper and hand sanitizer (or water for sink units), scheduled servicing, and final pickup of the unit upon completion of your rental period.

3. Can I rent a single portable restroom for just one day?

Absolutely. We offer one-day porta potty rentals in USA, which are perfect for weekend parties, family reunions, or any single-day event. We’ll handle the drop-off before your event starts and the pickup afterward, making the process completely hassle-free.

4. How far in advance do I need to book my porta potty rental?

For a single unit or a small number of portable toilets in USA, a week's notice is generally sufficient. However, for large events, weddings, or if you require a luxury restroom trailer, we recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season.

Bozeman (/ˈboʊzmən/ BOHZ-mən) is a city and the county seat of Gallatin County, Montana, United States. Located in southwest Montana, the 2020 census put Bozeman's population at 53,293 making it the fourth-largest city in Montana. It is the principal city of the Bozeman, Montana, Micropolitan Statistical Area, consisting of all of Gallatin County with a population of 118,960. It is the largest micropolitan statistical area in Montana, the fastest growing micropolitan statistical area in the United States in 2018, 2019 and 2020, as well as the second-largest of all Montana's statistical areas.
